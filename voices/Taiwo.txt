They arrange all our matches for us in this country, and indeed, manage most of our affairs, but then it must be remembered that we are strangers, as the title Persian denotes. Moreover, we belong to that division of the race that is called Blue Smokes, which means not that our fur is blue, for that would be ugly and loud, but that if you part it and look carefully at the roots, you will see that it is exactly the shade of blue that smoke is when you get a lot of it together.
